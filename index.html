Frequently Asked Questions

What is an eSIM?
An eSIM is a digital SIM that allows you to activate a cellular plan without a physical SIM card.
Which devices are compatible?
Most modern smartphones including iPhones XS+, Samsung Galaxy S20+, and Google Pixel support eSIMs.
Can I keep my existing number?
Yes, Smiley eSIM works as a secondary data line, keeping your original number active.
